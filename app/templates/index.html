Communicando

Who are we?

Communicando is the literary club of CBIT. It was started in 2007 with the aim to give students an opportunity to grow as individuals by enriching their soft skills.

Learn more

What is Literati?

Literati is the annual literary fest of CBIT, conducted by Communicando. It takes place in March every year, and every edition of Literati brings with it an exciting and rewarding array of events. There are a number of competitions conducted, and the events are a mix of time-tested classics and innovative new ideas.

Learn more

Literati 7.0

Literati 7.0 was one of the biggest editions of Literati which was held on March 9th and 10th. It witnessed the participation of over 300 students from colleges all over the city. The events organised ranged from exciting mystery solving events like "Who Dunnit" to the English debate "Dangal", various events such as "adaalat" were conducted in Hindi and Telugu as well. One of the highlights of Literati 7.0 was a microfiction workshop by Terribly Tiny tales. Fun game stalls and a photo booth was also set up where the participants relaxed and took pictures with funky props.

More than a literary club

CBITMUN

Back in 2011 CBIT MUN club was started by a group of debate enthusiasts with the motto to set up, encourage and endorse Model United Nations Conferences in the city of Hyderabad. It was Model United Nations Initiative for the first time of its level in the city of Hyderabad under the Literary Club of CBIT, Communicando.

Transcendent

Transcendent, the college newsletter of CBIT produces editions every year covering all the latest happenings in the college and around the world. Apart from the printed editions, Transcendent also has a weekly bulletin that covers every week's highlights on its Facebook page that is open to everybody.

The Visionaries

DR. A SANDHYA REDDY

Dr. A. Sandhya Reddy is the convenor of Communicando. She has a doctorate in English with specialization in American Literature and is an Associate Professor in the department of Maths and Humanities . With 24 years of teaching experience she has always been a student-friendly teacher. She is truly a friend, philosopher and guide to all the students. Communicando and all its other wings were established under her guidance and leadership. She always encourages her students to find their creative self and explore their latent potential. She has always given the students direction and encouraged them to take the club to scale great heights and today Communicando stands where it is only because of her guidance . New ideas and enthusiastic students are always welcomed in her cabin. She is the backbone of the club and a mother figure to all the students.

ANANYA MALIK

Ananya Malik is one of the Chief coordinators of Communicando. She’s an extremely energetic person who enjoys reading and chilling with her dog. She’s a hardworker who loves asking questions and finding answers to them, she is a lover of perfection and enjoys managing people. She does not believe in the concept of giving up no matter how difficult the situation maybe and there are few things she loves more than Communicando. She hopes to take Communicando to the greatest heights and provide highest quality literary events to students all over the city!
